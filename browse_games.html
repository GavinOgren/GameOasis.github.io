Gaming Library

Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a simple two-player game played on a 3x3 grid, where players take turns marking X or O to win three in a row.

Hangman

Hangman is a word-guessing game where one player thinks of a word, and the other player tries to guess it by suggesting letters. Incorrect guesses result in drawing parts of a stick figure until completion.

Memory

Each player takes turns flipping over two cards. If they match, the player keeps them and continues. If they don't match, the cards are flipped back over, and it's the next player's turn. The game ends when all cards are matched, and the player with the most pairs wins.
